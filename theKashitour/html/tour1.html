    Ancient Holy City Morning Boat / Hidden Pathways Tour

    The Ganges is the most sacred and venerated river in India. We do a morning boat ride on the Ganges to witness the ritualistic aspect of Hinduism. Watching people bathing & worshiping at the Ghats (River Bank) is one of the most extraordinary experiences of visit to India. Following the boat ride, we walk through the fascinating and winding lanes and many hidden bye-lanes on the banks of the river for a greater feel. Ancient Monuments, Temples, Houses, Colorful Shops, Tea Stalls, Pan Shops, Street Food stalls & Lassi Shops etc. are the highlights of the walk Tour.

    Duration- 4 - 5 Hours

    Prices- 1400/- INR Per Person.

    Additional person @ 1100/- INR
